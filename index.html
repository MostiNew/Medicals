``` Managing Checkup Data & Risk Insights This tool helps you turn raw checkup results into actionable health intelligence. Here’s how it works: · Patient & Visit Management: Use the top bar to select an existing patient or add a new one. The "New visit" button clears the form for a fresh entry, while the "Load" button in the history table lets you recall and edit any past visit. · Data Entry & Risk Scoring: Enter the checkup metrics (BMI, LDL, EF, etc.) into the left panel. Upon saving, the right panel automatically calculates a risk level (Low/Moderate/High) for each key variable and displays a Composite Risk Index (0–100) for a quick overall health snapshot. · Trend Tracking & Comparison: The history table lists all visits for the selected patient, showing changes (delta) from the previous visit with up/down arrows. Select a metric (e.g., LDL, BMI) from the dropdown to visualize its trend over time on the line chart, making it easy to spot progress or decline.
